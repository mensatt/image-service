This is the image service of Mensatt.

The following methods and endpoints are offered:

  • POST to /upload
  • POST to /submit/:id
  • POST to /approve/:id
  • GET to /image/:id
  • DELETE to /image/:id
  • POST to /unapprove/:id
  • POST to /rotate?id=<id>&angle=<angle>

For more information, take a look at the GitHub Repository
